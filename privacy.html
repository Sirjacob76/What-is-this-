Privacy Policy

Huh — “What is this?” · Last updated July 11, 2026

The short version: This app has no servers and no accounts. It does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information. Your photos, your questions, and your API key never pass through us — they go directly from your device to the AI provider you choose.

What the app does

Huh lets you take or upload a photo of something in your home, ask a question about it, and receive an AI-generated explanation. It runs entirely on your device in your browser or as an installed app.

Information we collect

None. The developer operates no server, database, analytics, or tracking of any kind. We have no ability to see your photos, questions, results, location, or usage.

Data that stays on your device

  • Your API key and provider preference are stored locally on your device (in browser storage) so you don't have to re-enter them. They never leave your device except to authenticate directly with the AI provider.
  • Your history of past diagnoses (the photo, question, and result) is saved locally on your device so you can revisit it. You can erase it any time with the Clear button; it is also removed if you uninstall the app or clear the app's data.

Data sent to third parties

When you request a diagnosis, the photo and your question are sent directly from your device to the AI provider you select, using your own API key:

  • Google Gemini (default) — governed by the Google Privacy Policy and Gemini API Terms.
  • Anthropic Claude (optional) — governed by the Anthropic Privacy Policy.

How that provider handles your data is subject to their policies and the terms of the account whose API key you use. We are not a party to that exchange and never receive a copy of it.

Camera and photos

The app uses your camera or photo library only to let you choose an image for a diagnosis, and only when you actively select one. Images are processed on your device and sent only to your chosen AI provider for that single request. We do not retain them.

Children

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
